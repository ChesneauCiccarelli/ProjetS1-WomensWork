About us

This website was created by Lilian Chesneau, a BUT student in the MMI department of the IUT of Belfort - Montbéliard, during the first semester of his training in the year 2021. This project is a pedagogical exercise carried out within the framework of the formation.

I proposed the discovery of feminist ideology through the work of women in society: their treatment, their actions against inequality, and the global development of the fight against sexism and segregation. In a definition of education and discovery, I hope that you have been able to learn about feminism and that the site has helped you understand more about this movement.
